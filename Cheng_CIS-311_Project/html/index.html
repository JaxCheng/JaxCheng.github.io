Nick was also in STP, meanwhile, Nick and Griffin went to the same high school in MA. Thus, Nick is on board. Finally, Jax is from China who had no idea what he is about to get in to before Matriculation day. Griffin and he roomed right next to each other so that the friendship started to grow. Griffin and Jack arranged a dinner to introduce Jax and Nick to each other. Jax felt Nick is quite guy and Nick also likes Jax.
